Voxel —
Voice That Excel

Voxel is a cutting-edge AI-powered platform designed to transform respiratory healthcare using the most accessible biometric: your voice. It is designed to assist clinicians and patients in the early detection, monitoring, and management of respiratory diseases—particularly Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD). This smarter diagnostic tools enables multimodal analysis of patient data to shape the future of digital health. Voxel brings the science of sound and intelligence of AI together for better outcomes.

We empower users through cough and voice analysis, offering early screening, personalized monitoring, and clinical insights — all from a simple audio sample. Voxel is your trusted partner at the intersection of medical science and deep technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Voxel a medical device?

Not yet. Currently, Voxel is a health companion app under clinical research. Regulatory approvals are part of our roadmap.

Do I need hospital equipment to use Voxel?

No. You only need a smartphone or computer with a microphone.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All recordings and health data are encrypted and stored following GDPR and NHS data standards.

Can Voxel diagnose diseases?

Voxel provides AI-driven health insights and alerts. Diagnosis can only be made through a licensed healthcare provider.

Can I share results with my doctor?

Yes, easily. You can export reports or link your clinician to your profile.
